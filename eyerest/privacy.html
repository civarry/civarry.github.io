EyeRest Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

The short version

EyeRest collects no personal data, transmits nothing to any server, requires no account, and works entirely offline. The app contains no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking SDKs.

EyeRest is built and maintained by CJ Carito (Christian Joy Collano Carito) as an independent developer. This policy explains what EyeRest does — and does not — do with your data.

What EyeRest stores on your device

The following are stored only on your device and never transmitted anywhere:

  • Your settings (focus interval, break duration, quiet hours, etc.) — via Android's SharedPreferences.
  • Your completed break logs (date, duration, status) — in a local SQLite database for the in-app daily and weekly stats.

Uninstalling EyeRest deletes all of this permanently.

Permissions EyeRest requests

  • Notifications — to show eye-break reminders.
  • Display over other apps (optional, opt-in) — only used when "Strict break lock" is enabled, and only during an active 20-second break. Dismisses automatically when the break ends.
  • Schedule exact alarms — to fire the eye-break reliably at the end of each focus interval.
  • Vibrate — for the gentle haptic cue at break start.
  • Wake lock and Foreground service — to keep the alarm sound playing and the break overlay visible during an active break.

EyeRest does not request: camera, microphone, location, contacts, storage, phone state, SMS, or any other sensitive permission.

Third-party services

EyeRest uses no third-party services. No Google Analytics, no Firebase, no crash reporting, no remote configuration, no ads.

Children

EyeRest is not designed for or directed at children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect data from anyone, regardless of age.

Changes to this policy

If EyeRest ever adds a feature that changes what data is handled (for example, an optional tip-jar in-app purchase using Google Play Billing), this policy will be updated and the "Last updated" date above will change.
